TERRY & BATH LINEN DIVISION

Complete Towel & Bath Solutions

From hospitality-grade towels to institutional textiles, TajPak delivers end-to-end terry solutions engineered for softness, durability, absorbency, and global export compliance.

Towels

Our premium towel range is engineered using high GSM cotton yarns to deliver exceptional softness, absorbency, and durability. Designed for hotels, retailers, and global distributors.

  • • GSM Range: 400–800
  • • 100% Cotton & Organic Cotton options
  • • Double-stitched reinforced edges
  • • Export-grade quality control (AQL 2.5)

Bathrobes

Luxurious bathrobes tailored for hotels, spas, and premium hospitality brands. Designed for comfort, warmth, and elegance after bathing or spa treatments.

  • • Terry, waffle & velour fabric options
  • • Shawl collar, kimono & hooded styles
  • • Custom embroidery & branding available
  • • Lightweight & heavyweight variants

Beach & Pool Towels

Designed for resorts, beaches, and luxury pools, our beach towels combine vibrant aesthetics with quick-dry performance and sand resistance.

  • • Lightweight quick-dry cotton blends
  • • Fade-resistant reactive printing
  • • Oversized luxury formats available
  • • Ideal for resorts & hospitality brands

Institutional Towels

Built for high-volume usage in hospitals, gyms, and institutions. These towels are engineered for repeated industrial washing cycles without quality loss.

  • • High durability industrial weave
  • • Bleach & chemical wash resistant
  • • Bulk supply for hospitals & gyms
  • • Long lifecycle performance

Bath Mats & Terry Slippers

Complete bathroom comfort solutions including absorbent bath mats and soft terry slippers designed for hotels, spas, and luxury hospitality environments.

  • • Anti-slip high absorbency mats

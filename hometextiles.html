HOME TEXTILE DIVISION

Complete Home Textile Solutions

From bedding essentials to decorative furnishings, TajPak delivers premium home textile solutions engineered for comfort, durability, and global retail standards.

Bedsheets & Flat Sheets

We are a specialist exporter of bedsheets and flat sheets, offering premium bedding solutions in 100% cotton, linen, and blended poly-cotton or cotton fabrics. Our products are available in dyed or printed styles, with customizable piping to match any design specification.

Our luxury Bedding Linens are engineered to align with your stitching requirements. The fabric features a smooth, soft texture and is supplied in solid white, solid color, or printed variations.

All products are fully customizable. We incorporate blended fabrics as required and offer a range of finish categories across the following thread counts: TC 130, TC 180, TC 200, TC 250, and TC 300.

Available Fabric Constructs:

  • • Standard plain weave
  • • Brushed finish for thermal comfort
  • • High-sheen satin weave
  • • Lightweight breathable weave
  • • Satin weave with ribbed stripe detailing

Fitted Sheets

Premium fitted sheets designed for a secure fit and long-lasting mattress protection. Available in T-128 to T-300 thread counts using 100% Cotton, Poly-Cotton (52:48 / 65:35), and CVC.

Ideal for both retail and institutional use, with easy-care fabrics that maintain freshness through repeated washing cycles.

Offered in solid colors with custom options available. White remains the preferred choice for its clean, versatile appeal.

Weave Options:

  • • Percale
  • • Sateen
  • • Twill
  • • Jersey
  • • Dobby
  • • Patterned

Duvet & Quilt Covers

Extend the life of duvet inserts and reduce maintenance costs with duvet and quilt covers designed to protect against body oils, moisture, and spills — keeping duvets cleaner for longer.

Available in T-144 to T-400 thread counts, using 100% Cotton, Poly-Cotton (50:50 / 60:40 / 65:35), and CVC, with weave options including Percale, Sateen, Twill, Dobby, and Patterned.

Offered in Twin to King sizes with zipper or corner-tie closures for a secure fit. Ideal for institutional use with easy-laundry shades like white and beige.

Additional Features:

  • • Zipper or tie closure options
  • • Water-resistant finishes
  • • Stain-release treatments
  • • Durable for high-use environments

Pillow Covers & Pillow Cases

Keep your pillows fresher, cleaner, and longer-lasting with our premium pillow protectors and covers. A high-quality pillow protector creates an effective barrier against moisture, stains, body oils, and daily wear and tear — helping you preserve the comfort and support of your favorite pillow for many years.

What we offer:

  • • Materials: 100% Cotton, Polyester, Satin, Flannel, CVC and PC blends
  • • Design options: Printed, Dyed, or White — reversible or simple styles
  • • Premium fabrics: Woven textures, Jacquards, and Dobby designs in cotton and poly-cotton
  • • Specialized covers: Institutional and hospital-grade for frequent laundering and high-use environments
  • • Custom throw pillow covers: Manufactured to your tech pack — custom designs, sizes, and closures
  • • Flexible ordering: Supplied individually or as part of complete bed sheet sets

Cushion Covers & Cushion Cases

Protect your cushions from germs, dirt, and buildup with our high-quality cushion covers and protectors. Our covers create a reliable barrier that prevents dead skin, dust, and bacteria from penetrating deep into the cushion core — keeping your cushions cleaner, more hygienic, and longer-lasting.

Fully customized to your specifications:

  • • Material composition – 100% Cotton, Polyester, Blended Cotton/Poly (52:48 or 65:35), CVC, Microfiber, or other synthetics
  • • Filling and weight – Your choice based on desired firmness and feel
  • • Printing methods – Digital print, reactive print, or pigment print for vibrant, long-lasting designs
  • • Thread count – Ranging from T-128 to T-300
  • • Sizing – Any custom dimensions you need

Material benefits at a glance:

  • • Natural cotton and cotton-polyester blends – Easy to wash, simple to clean, and hold their shape well over time
  • • Synthetic options like microfiber and polyester – Affordable, durable, and available in a wide range of attractive finishes

Curtains & Draperies

Stay ahead in interior design with our curtain sets, crafted to meet both current and evolving market trends. Whether for residential, hospitality, or commercial spaces, our curtains combine style, function, and quality.

Fabric options for every need:

  • • Elegant luxury fabrics – Silk, Damask, Suede, and Chenille for high-end, sophisticated interiors
  • • Practical high-performance fabrics – PVC for moisture resistance, easy cleaning, and durability in high-humidity areas
  • • Custom prints and solids – Dyed or printed fabrics made from scratch based on your exact design requirements

Designed by window type, usage, and interior style — blackout, sheer, thermal, or decorative — we tailor accordingly.

OUR SOURCING SOLUTIONS

End-to-End Sourcing Solutions

A structured, ERP-integrated workflow that manages every stage of your textile supply chain with precision and transparency.

Vendor Selection

Critical sourcing decision impacting quality, cost, delivery & compliance.

Structured requirement-based vendor filtering system.

Vendor selection is the most critical decision in any sourcing engagement. A miscalculation at this stage propagates through every subsequent phase — quality, cost, delivery, and compliance. At TajPak, we treat vendor selection as a disciplined, data-driven process — not a transactional handoff. We begin by capturing your complete requirements: product specifications, acceptable quality limits (AQL), required certifications (OEKO-TEX, amfori BSCI, Sedex, GOTS), volume projections, and target landed costs. This requirement profile becomes the filter through which every potential vendor is assessed.

Merchandising & Designing

Converts buyer vision into technical factory specs.

Covers home textiles & apparel development.

Product development and design interpretation are where sourcing success begins. A well-specified product — with clear materials, construction standards, and quality benchmarks — eliminates ambiguity, reduces sampling cycles, and prevents costly production errors. At TajPak, we bridge the gap between your product vision and factory execution. Our merchandising and design function translates buyer requirements into technical specifications that manufacturers can execute precisely — across home textiles (bedding, towels, curtains, throws) and apparel (casual wear, activewear, knitwear, woven garments).

Market Research

Continuous global market intelligence system

Tracks fairs, agencies & supplier networks

Sourcing decisions without market intelligence are guesses, not strategy. At TajPak, market research is a continuous intelligence function — tracking pricing trends, raw material fluctuations, supplier capacity, and regulatory changes across home textiles and apparel. Our intelligence ecosystem spans 7+ global trade fairs (Heimtextil, Première Vision, Magic Las Vegas, Texworld, Intertextile, Munich Fabric Start, Canton Fair), raw material exchanges (ICAC, NY/ICE), forecasting agencies (WGSN, Textile World, Just-Style), regulatory bodies (OEKO-TEX, amfori BSCI, Sedex, GOTS), on-ground supplier data from Pakistan's textile hubs, and global sourcing databases. The result? Data-backed decisions, negotiation leverage, early trend access, and a supply chain that anticipates market shifts.

Production Follow Up

Milestone-based production tracking

ERP-based real-time visibility

At TajPak, production follow up is a disciplined, milestone-driven process. We track every stage from raw material allocation through cutting, stitching, finishing, packing, and container loading — ensuring no hidden delays and complete visibility. Inline quality checks verify production standards during manufacturing, not just at the end, so issues are caught early and rework is minimized. Our ERP-integrated system provides real-time status updates against agreed timelines, delivering proactive alerts and eliminating surprises. Daily vendor coordination enables faster problem resolution, while any identified delay or quality issue triggers immediate corrective action planning — keeping production firmly on track.

Quality Assurance

Multi-layer inspection system

Includes SGS testing & AQL 4

At TajPak, quality assurance is a multi-layered framework applied across home textiles and apparel. We conduct pre-production inspections — vetting vendors, verifying materials, and aligning processes to stop problems before production starts. Inline inspections during cutting, stitching, and finishing catch issues early, reducing rework. Final inspection enforces AQL 4 standards (max 4% defect tolerance), ensuring consistent, verifiable quality. Pre-shipment verification checks finished goods before container loading, giving you confidence before shipment leaves. We also facilitate SGS testing — independent third-party validation for audit-ready compliance and buyer confidence.

Shipping & Logistics

End-to-end freight management

Full export documentation handling

At TajPak, we manage end-to-end shipping and logistics across home textiles and apparel. Our freight management covers ocean (FCL/LCL), air, and multi-modal solutions under FOB or CNF terms — delivering cost-effective, reliable transport. We handle all export documentation — BL (Bill of Lading), CRO (Certificate of Origin), invoices, packing lists, and export declarations — ensuring no customs delays and audit-ready paperwork. Route optimization balances cost and time efficiency for faster delivery and lower freight costs. Real-time shipment tracking provides proactive updates from factory to destination — no guessing. And our customs support aligns documentation with destination country requirements for smooth clearance and no regulatory fines.

After Sales Service

Post-shipment monitoring system

Continuous improvement loop

At TajPak, after sales service begins the moment your container is dispatched. We monitor shipment arrival, customs clearance, and warehouse receipt — confirming your order arrived as expected. Any discrepancy — quantity, quality, or documentation — triggers immediate investigation and fast, fair resolution. We maintain a structured feedback loop, capturing lessons learned from every order to drive continuous improvement and fewer repeat issues. Performance analysis reviews product data, returns, and customer feedback — enabling smarter sourcing decisions for future orders. The result is a long-term partnership built on structured follow-up, accountability, and trust — a partner who improves order after order

Supply Chain Integration

Unified sourcing ecosystem

Closed-loop improvement system

At TajPak, supply chain integration means connecting every function into one unified workflow. Our unified data architecture creates a single source of truth for orders, specifications, inspections, and shipments — eliminating conflicting information and ensuring full traceability. Seamless function handoffs ensure no information is lost between vendor selection, production, logistics, or after sales — delivering faster execution and fewer errors. Real-time visibility through ERP-tracked milestones across every stage enables proactive decisions and eliminates surprises. Closed-loop improvement feeds after sales lessons back into vendor selection and quality planning — driving continuous improvement and fewer repeat issues. And dual-category integration manages home textiles and apparel under one framework — one partner, one standard, complete visibility.

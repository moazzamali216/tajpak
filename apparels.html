Henley Shirts

Originating from the traditional uniform of rowers in Henley-on-Thames, England, the Henley shirt represents a refined hybrid design that combines the comfort of a T-shirt, the structured collarless neckline of a polo, and the simplicity of a pullover.

As exporters of premium Henley shirts, we supply high-quality, comfortable, and versatile garments for both men and women in bulk quantities. All products are manufactured using advanced machinery and high-grade materials, fully compliant with customer-provided specifications.

Product details:

  • • Available finishes – Brushing, Peaching, and Silicone Wash
  • • Fabric enhancement – Spandex may be incorporated for flexibility and improved shape retention
  • • Customization – Full customization in color, sizing, and packaging per buyer requirements
  • • Quality commitment – Rigorous attention to product detail and optimal manufacturing standards ensuring consistent delivery

Hoodies & Sweatshirts

Sweatpants & Joggers

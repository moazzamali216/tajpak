OUR STORY

Global Sourcing Partner in Textiles & Apparel

“TajPak was founded in Lyallpur in 2001 with a simple belief: global buyers deserve a sourcing partner who treats their supply chain like their own business. Over two decades, we’ve grown into a complete textile solutions provider—serving brands, retailers, and wholesalers worldwide. Our edge comes from deep vendor partnerships, efficient internal systems, and a dedicated team. But what truly sets us apart is our proprietary ERP integration—a centralized digital backbone that connects order management, inventory tracking, quality control checkpoints, logistics coordination, and compliance documentation into a single, transparent workflow. From raw material sourcing to final shipment, every transaction, inspection report, and shipment milestone is accessible. This means fewer errors, faster decision-making, and complete accountability for our clients. Committed to innovation, sustainability, and long-term relationships, TajPak continues to adapt to market shifts—so you don’t have to.”

Why TajPak?

Strategic Vendor Sourcing

Pre-vetted mills, ERP-tracked performance, and dual-category sourcing under one roof. Our SWOT-driven vendor assessment ensures you get partners with the right strengths, minimal weaknesses, real opportunities, and no hidden threats — delivering consistency, cost efficiency, and full traceability.

Product Development

From concept to sample, we turn your ideas into market-ready products aligned with global trends, buyer specifications, and international certifications like OEKO-TEX, BSCI, and GOTS — ensuring faster sampling, fewer revisions, and production-ready results.

Market Insights

Data-driven market analysis and strategic pricing intelligence. We track raw material trends, regional cost fluctuations, and supplier behavior — enabling better negotiation, accurate budgeting, and competitive pricing before market shifts.

Production Management

End-to-end production coordination including vendor scheduling, quality checkpoints, and logistics alignment. We monitor every stage — from raw material to container loading — resolving bottlenecks early to ensure smooth execution and on-time delivery.

Quality Assurance

Pre-production to final inspection with strict quality protocols. We follow AQL 4 standards, verify GSM accuracy, and facilitate SGS third-party testing — ensuring every shipment meets international benchmarks before dispatch.

Logistics & Export Support

End-to-end logistics and trade compliance including freight consolidation, route optimization, and documentation such as BL and Certificate of Origin. Structured under FOB or CNF terms, we ensure predictable, cost-efficient, and frictionless delivery.
